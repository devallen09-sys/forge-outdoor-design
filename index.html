Our Approach

"Every pour, every finish, every detail — made as if it bears our name forever."

Forge Outdoor Design was built on a simple belief: the outdoor spaces of a fine home deserve the same level of craft and intention as the interior. We specialize in premium concrete design and hardscaping for homeowners in the Gadsden area who want something beyond the ordinary.

Every piece is formed and finished on-site — not shipped from a warehouse, not subcontracted to the lowest bidder. You work directly with the craftsman from first site visit to final seal coat.

As one of Northeast Alabama's only certified resin bound surfacing installers, we're also proud to bring a truly distinctive finish option to the region — durable, permeable, and unlike anything else you'll find locally.

How It Works

From concept to completion

01

Consultation

We meet at your property, discuss your vision, assess the space, and explore material and finish options suited to your home.

02

Design & Proposal

We prepare a detailed proposal with scope, materials, timeline, and investment — no surprises, no hidden costs.

03

Fabrication

Work begins. Concrete is cast, cured, and finished in our shop before delivery, or poured on-site for larger installations.

04

Installation & Finish

Final installation, sealing, and cleanup. We walk you through care and maintenance before we consider a project complete.
